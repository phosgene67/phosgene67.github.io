About Me

I'm Sami Chowdhury, an EEE (Electrical and Electronic Engineering) student with a passion for robotics and embedded systems. My journey has been driven by curiosity about how machines perceive and interact with the physical world.

With hands-on experience in designing and building robotic systems, I specialize in creating intelligent solutions that combine hardware design, firmware development, and control algorithms. From underwater ROVs to autonomous vehicles, I enjoy tackling complex engineering challenges with innovative approaches.

My expertise spans across PCB design, microcontroller programming, sensor integration, and real-time systems. I'm particularly interested in bringing AI and machine learning into robotics to create smarter, more autonomous platforms.
